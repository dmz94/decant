Australian Government Guide to Policy Impact Analysis

17 February 2023

This Guide helps policy makers reflect on how policy can affect people, businesses and community as well as its broader economic and competition impacts.

The Government’s rigorous approach to policy making seeks to ensure that the costs and benefits of new policy are understood from all angles.

The Government’s Impact Analysis framework will ensure decision makers are provided with the necessary evidence base to make the decisions that will best support a stronger economy and guarantee the essentials Australians rely on to prosper.

It is an expectation of the Government that policy makers aim to prepare an Impact Analysis (IA) that is assessed as exemplary.

The new Australian Government Guide to Policy Impact Analysis comes into effect from 1 March 2023. The former Australian Government Guide to Policy Impact Analysis is also available below, along with a summary of the changes.
