Khan Academy Goes Way Beyond Math: New Courses Launch for 2024 School Year in STEM, Humanities, Computer Science, and More

May 29, 2025August 6, 2024

You may be surprised to see these new courses and resources on Khan Academy. Just in time for the new school year, the Khan Academy team has unveiled a lineup of exciting, free content updates that you’ll want to use in your classroom or at home. 

These include hands-on science investigative activities, an Introduction to Python course, a Constitution 101 course, TEKS-aligned math and science courses for students and teachers in Texas, and more!

Think Khan Academy is just for math? Wait until you see what we launched for the new school year. 

Dive into our latest offerings and get ready for an engaging and innovative year of learning!

Science 

High school biology, chemistry, and physics
Our new high school science courses are aligned to the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and include a rich set of learning resources, including brand-new videos, articles, and practice exercises. We’ve taken everything you loved about our previous courses and enhanced them, creating an engaging, standards-aligned journey that sparks curiosity and fuels scientific discovery. 

Get free hands-on investigative science activities. 
With support from the Adobe Foundation, we’ve developed engaging, hands-on high school chemistry and physics activities designed by experts to promote creative thinking. 

Humanities 

Reading and vocabulary for grades 6-10
Khan Academy is excited to unveil our new and enhanced English Language Arts (ELA) courses for 9th and 10th grade. These Common Core-aligned courses are designed to provide comprehensive support for mastering reading and vocabulary skills through engaging multimedia resources, including videos, articles, and interactive exercises. Watch for more ELA courses coming soon in September 2024. 

Constitution 101 course
Constitution 101 offers engaging lessons and interactive content to help students understand the US Constitution. It’s perfectly aligned with educational standards and easy to integrate. 

Computer science

Our new Intro to Computer Science course teaches the fundamentals of programming in Python. This course is packed with innovative features and engaging content, making it an ideal addition to beginner computer science classrooms.

Financial literacy

Our 16-unit Financial Literacy course covers topics from budgeting and saving to taxes and tax forms that are packed with videos, articles, and exercises that all meet state standards. Just in time for back to school, the course got a major upgrade with four brand-new units and two additional real-world videos! Explore the new units: employment, banking, car buying, and housing.

Courses exclusively for Texas students & educators 

Khan Academy and ExxonMobil Foundation have partnered up to open the doors to STEM education in Texas! Thanks to the generous funding from ExxonMobil Foundation, we are thrilled to introduce our new math and science courses for the 2024-25 school year, which have been thoughtfully aligned with the Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS) standards. 

New TEKS-aligned math courses
Spanning 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th grades, as well as Algebra 1, these courses are designed to provide robust, comprehensive support that’s tailored to meet Lone Star State-specific educational goals. 

New TEKS-aligned science courses
Our new high school courses in biology, chemistry, and physics are thoughtfully aligned with the TEKS science standards. These courses are designed to provide robust, comprehensive support that’s tailored to meet Texas-specific educational goals.
